# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 19 May 2026**

Rokid Remote ("the app") is a free, open source app developed by Inplov.

## Data We Collect

The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

## Bluetooth

The app uses Bluetooth to communicate directly between your Android phone and your Rokid AR glasses. No Bluetooth data is sent to any third party.

## Advertising

The app displays banner ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and use cookies to serve personalised ads. Please refer to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for details on how Google handles this data.

## Local Storage

The app stores browsing history and bookmarks locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted anywhere.

## Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

## Contact

For any questions, open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/Inplov/rokid-browser).
